Eggs Recipe

Description

In order to make the best eggs its a simple propcess you will only need butter , Eggs and cheese

Ingridients

  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps to making the best eggs

  1. Heat A Pan at medium heat
  2. Place Butter in the pan
  3. Crack Eggs inside the pan
  4. Scramble eggs inside the pan and add a pinch of butter
  5. When the eggs start to form add the cheese and turn the heat low
  6. Keep Scrambling until it is at the consistancy that you like
