Oodles Of Noodles

Description

A simple quick meal of Ramen

Ingridients

  • Boiling water
  • A pack of your favorite brand of noodles I Prefer Ramen

Steps to making noodles

  1. Place water in a pot for 3 minutes or until the water starts boiling
  2. Once the water starts boiling place the noodles inside of the water for about 3 minutes
  3. When the noodles reaches the consistancy that you likes take them out and pour into a bowl
  4. Place the noodles inside the bowl in season to your likening
  5. You can add chicken , egg , steak to your ramen to enhance the flavor
