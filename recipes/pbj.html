Peanut Butter & Jelly

Description

Penut Butter & Jelly is a quick and great snack

Ingridients

  • Bread
  • Jelly
  • Peanut butter

Steps to makining PB&J

  1. Place two pieces of bread, peanut butter and jelly on a table
  2. Get your self a spoon or a butter knife out
  3. Apply jelly to one piece of the bread
  4. Apply peanut butter to another
  5. Place both pieces of bread together and enjoy
